Welcome to the EHR System

A platform designed to help doctors efficiently manage patient data.

About the System

Our Electronic Health Record (EHR) system is designed to streamline patient data management for healthcare professionals. It allows doctors to efficiently manage patient records, create new patient profiles, track medical history, and perform various operations in real-time.

This system provides an intuitive interface for doctors to interact with patient data, providing capabilities such as CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations.
